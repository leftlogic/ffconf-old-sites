Conference Details

The Day - 20th November 2009

Registration starts at 9am until 9:45am. The event run until 5:45pm, with 30 minute breaks at around 11am and 4pm and 90 minutes for lunch. We won't be providing lunch, but here's some ideas for you.

Please find below details which will provide you with useful and important information to make sure your visit to Full Frontal JavaScript Conference is a memorable experience.

Directions

How to get there: http://icanhaz.com/ff-directions

Full Frontal JavaScript Conference is being held at the historic Duke of York’s Picturehouse in Brighton. It is centrally located on Preston Circus, less than 15 minutes walk from Brighton train station. Trains run regularly from Gatwick Airport, London Bridge and London Victoria stations. We recommend using http://www.traintimes.org.uk for up to date timetables. Hotel recommendations can be found on the Full Frontal website: http://2009.full-frontal.org/details

Registration

Registration opens at 9am. You must bring a form of identification to pick up your delegate programme (bank card, driving licence etc). Please let us know if you are unable to attend, we will accept replacements if we are informed prior to the day.

Programme

The most up to date schedule and speaker abstracts can be found on the website, http://2009.full-frontal.org

Please feel free to photo or video any part of the conference. Please use the hash tag #fullfrontal09 if you do upload anything so everyone else can see it too, and feel free to follow the Twitter account @fullfrontalconf for the latest info on the event.

Refreshments

Tea, Coffee and Danishes will be available from 9am in the upstairs bar and the ground floor. Further refreshments will be available from the same locations at the mid morning and mid afternoon breaks. Please note lunch is not included but the programme includes local recommendations and maps, some of which can be found here: http://icanhaz.com/ff-lunch

After Party

Please join us at the After Party at Oh So Social from 7pm-2am, directions will be included in the programme, also available here: http://icanhaz.com/ohso

Pre conference

If you’re coming down to Brighton on Thursday evening you may want to join in some pre-conference burgers and a few beers, more information available here: http://icanhaz.com/ffburger, please mark yourself as coming using Upcoming so that the numbers are correct for the burgers.

Post conference

We aim to have the sound recordings taken from the day online afterwards so you are able to re-listen to the conference. Please keep an eye on the website and our Twitter feed for updates.

Health and Safety

Please report any lost property to the registration desk. Left Logic and Duke of York Picturehouse takes no responsibility for delegate’s lost property so we ask you to be vigilant at all times.

The venue has at least one first-aider on duty at all times who can be contacted via the registration desk.

The venue is fully accessible. Left Logic would like to ensure that all delegates are able to have access therefore should you have any special requests please contact me.

If we can be of any further assistance, you can email events@leftlogic.com.

We look forward to meeting you at the conference.
